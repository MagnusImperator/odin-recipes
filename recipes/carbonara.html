Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian dish made from pasta, egg, pancetta, black pepper and grated cheese

Ingredients

  • 200g dried or fresh spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g butter
  • 100g pancetta, guincale or bacon lardons
  • 100g parmeggiano or similar hard cheese
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring water to the boil, add salt, stir and then add pasta.
  2. Add the pancetta to a hot frying pan, cook until crispy and fat is rendered.
  3. Whisk eggs and most of cheese together. Add a lot of black pepper
  4. Add cooked pasta to the pan, making sure to coat with fat. Remove the pan from the heat and pour in your egg mixture, adding pasta water to loosen if necessary.
  5. Serve with additional black pepper and cheese to taste.
