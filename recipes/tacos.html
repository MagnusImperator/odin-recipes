Tacos

Description

A fun and flavourful Mexican family favourite

Ingredients

  • Small tortillas
  • Beef, pork, chicken or prawns
  • 300g guacamole
  • Sharp cheese e.g. feta
  • Salsa of choice
  • Lime wedge

Steps

  1. Cook meat, with any seasonings desired.
  2. Warm tortillas in a pan until lightly browned.
  3. Add guacamole and salsa. Layer your meat and cheese on top.
  4. Squeeze lime wedge over assembled taco.
